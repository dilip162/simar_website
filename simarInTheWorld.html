SIMAR IN THE WORLD

Success Stories

Simar Group is deeply linked to its territory and is proud to be able to represent Made in Italy quality in the world.

Aware of its unique craftsmanship, Simar has undertaken important commercial, productive and technological investments with which it has conquered foreign markets.

“INTERNATIONALISATION”: ONE CONCEPT, DIFFERENT MEANINGS

Simar Group faced the internationalization process as a challenge that did not only involve the realities with which it came into contact. Simar has interpreted foreign markets as a way to involve consumers and local partners by being the first to enter the culture of their places, evaluating traditions and behaviors until perceiving lifestyles, tastes and real needs.

An ability that starts from afar, from the desire to establish human relationships and bonds that also allow us to enhance and empower local skills and professionalism.
Sincere values ​​that have allowed us not only to establish ourselves on the most interesting markets but also in the most difficult and demanding ones, thanks also to an extremely high quality product and service.

The internationalization strategy was born from a careful analysis which, first, made it possible to identify the geographical areas potentially involved and then to start an intense and widespread targeted activity of visits and contacts with operators and partners.
