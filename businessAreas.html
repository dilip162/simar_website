SIMAR GROUP SRL

The Birth of the Group

The Group was born from the experience and entrepreneurial spirit of the Valle family who have passionately handed down the art of custom-made furnishings for three generations.

The path of constant growth, still linked to its tradition and its territory, evolves rapidly thanks to the great craftsmanship and the continuous search for materials of the highest quality and quality.

A solid philosophy that has allowed the Group to establish itself on the international market, receiving widespread recognition in the supply of furnishings for homes, accommodation facilities and commercial offices.

History

In the mid-80s the company moved to Monte Urano in the province of Fermo, a location recognized worldwide as a center of quality and Made in Italy craftsmanship. After the growth that took place on the national market, at the beginning of the 90s the company, aware of its strength, expanded and headed towards foreign markets, obtaining its first great successes in the United Kingdom and in particular in the demanding London area.

The company, strengthened by the feedback obtained from its high quality standards, immediately understood the potential of international markets and decided to invest in an intense research and development activity that enriches Simar Arredamenti with cutting-edge technologies and new knowledge that allow the the company achieves results of absolute excellence in the creation of original and unique furnishings.

The great attention paid to service, punctual delivery and the integrity of values ​​and human relationships between company and customers have allowed rapid expansion into further high-value markets: European Union, Russia, Hong Kong, Vietnam, Singapore, Indonesia and Ethiopia.

The desire to maintain and respect its tradition of all-Italian quality and craftsmanship combined with the professionalism and ability to anticipate the most particular requests of global markets, Simar has grown into an important Italian group in continuous expansion, today specialized in different business areas :

» Simar Arredamenti - Creation of quality and tailor-made furnishings for homes, offices, commercial premises and accommodation facilities.

» S[i]mart - Development of innovative and technologically advanced furniture projects that integrate Artificial Intelligence solutions.

» Simar Investment Group - Dedicated to the specific "ecobonus" sector for which a synergistic complex of specialized partners has been created.

» Simar Lab Tech - It is the place where all initiatives come to life: project management, customization concepts, development of services and relationships with customers, management of production lines.

» Research and Development - Formed by a team that boasts over 25 years of experience in designing innovative solutions in the artificial intelligence and new technologies sector.

» Green Economy - The area where sustainable Architecture and Design become an increasingly concrete reality to support the design concept of architects and designers.

The Group is our Family

Simar has always paid particular attention to human relationships, aware that the synergy, harmony and value of the Group's members are a successful formula with which to achieve even greater objectives.
